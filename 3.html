Where the Bison Roam

If you drive up towards the Sandia Mountains, perhaps towards the trailhead for La Luz, you will drive through reservation land, and may be lucky enough to spot some bison grazing next to the road.

This video was taken in Cimarronn, a small village with a population under 1,000. Despite it's small size, its location in northern New Mexico, stunning geographic features, and wildlife make it a tourist attraction for those looking for culture and nature in the southwest.
